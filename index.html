Our Story

Founded in 2020, PERFECT TEN emerged from a simple belief: fashion should be accessible, sustainable, and empowering. What started as a small boutique has grown into a global community of fashion enthusiasts who share our values of quality, ethics, and style.

Our Journey

Our founders, Sarah and Michael, noticed a gap in the market for affordable yet high-quality fashion that didn't compromise on ethical standards. They traveled the world, partnering with artisans and manufacturers who shared their vision of responsible fashion.

Our Mission

We're committed to creating beautiful clothing that makes you feel confident while making a positive impact on the world. Every piece in our collection is carefully selected for its quality, style, and the story behind its creation.

Quality First

Every piece is carefully crafted with premium materials and attention to detail. We believe in creating clothing that lasts, not just for a season, but for years to come.

Ethical Manufacturing

We work exclusively with manufacturers who provide fair wages, safe working conditions, and respect for workers' rights. Our supply chain is transparent and traceable.

Environmental Responsibility

From sustainable materials to eco-friendly packaging, we're constantly working to reduce our environmental footprint and promote circular fashion.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

Sustainability isn't just a buzzword for us—it's at the core of everything we do. From the materials we choose to the way we package and ship our products, we're committed to minimizing our environmental impact.

Sustainable Materials

We prioritize organic cotton, recycled polyester, and other eco-friendly materials. 80% of our collection is made from sustainable fabrics.

Ethical Production

All our manufacturing partners are certified for fair labor practices and environmental standards. We conduct regular audits to ensure compliance.

Carbon Neutral Shipping

We offset 100% of our shipping emissions through verified carbon offset programs and use recyclable packaging materials.

Circular Fashion

Our take-back program allows customers to return worn items for recycling. We also offer repair services to extend product life.

Water Conservation

Our manufacturing processes use 50% less water than industry standards through innovative dyeing and finishing techniques.

Transparency

We publish annual sustainability reports detailing our progress and goals. Transparency builds trust and accountability.

Our 2024 Goals

  • Achieve 100% sustainable materials across all product lines
  • Reduce packaging waste by 30%
  • Launch our clothing rental program
  • Partner with 5 new certified sustainable suppliers
  • Plant 10,000 trees through our reforestation program

How to Measure

Chest/Bust

Measure around the fullest part of your chest/bust, keeping the tape measure level and snug but not tight.

Waist

Measure around your natural waistline, which is typically the narrowest part of your torso.

Hips

Measure around the fullest part of your hips, typically about 8 inches below your waistline.

Length

For tops, measure from the highest point of your shoulder down to where you want the garment to end.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I determine my size?
We provide detailed size charts for each product. You can find the size guide by clicking the "Size Guide" link on any product page. We recommend measuring yourself and comparing with our charts for the best fit.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy for all unworn items with original tags. Items must be in original condition. Return shipping is free for exchanges, and we charge a small fee for refunds.
How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping (1-2 business days) is available for an additional fee. International shipping takes 7-14 business days depending on location.
Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. You can see the exact cost and estimated delivery time at checkout.
How can I track my order?
Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package on our website or the carrier's website.
